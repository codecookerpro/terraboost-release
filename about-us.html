For over a decade, Terraboost has been connecting businesses with potential customers through sponsored wellness.

Terraboost has partnered with more than 1,000 malls, 5000+ supermarkets, 5,000+ drugstores, airports, and other strategic locations to provide welcome center/cart wipes for the health and wellness of their billions of guests. Align your business with a Wellness Kiosk that shoppers use and appreciate so your brand can achieve top of mind recall. Our billboards reach an impressive 3.9 billion consumers per month. Brands can engage with their target audience nationally, regionally, or locally by selecting the advertising networks and/or locations that matter most. Join the thousands of satisfied brands who continue to leverage this engaging media platform that Nielsen research confirms 63% ad recall, 94% positive brand opinion, and a 36% lift in purchase intent.

Our Exciting Journey

The Start

Company founded by brothers Brian and Brett Morrison.

The Foundation

Multiple hand sanitizing billboard dispensers designed & patented and deployed to retail locations in top markets.

Protecting Shopping Hands

Mall network established across the USA - driving momentum as network reaches 10,000 wellness kiosks.

Growth

Manhattan office opened, 50 overall employees. Terraboost launches digital hand sanitizing billboards in several countries.

Supermarket Channel

The company reaches 100 Million impressions per year in the USA and begins supermarket channel growth.

Product Expansion

Terraboost launches hand sanitizing wipe billboard to add to product arsenal and better serve venue partners and consumers alike.

New Office, New Channel

Chicagoland office and warehouse logistics center launched to better serve venue partners. Terraboost enters the drugstore channel as a source of continued advertising network growth. Media network expands to 40,000 locations nationwide.

Going Hyperlocal

To better service a wider customer base - hyper-local division launched to connect local advertisers to their communities.

Specialty Retail Expansion

Additional specialty retail partnerships and grocery partners added to the wellness media network. Ad network reaches 50,000 wellness billboards nationwide.

Expanding Logistics

Multiple additional warehouses open to support logistics for expanded media network partner locations and venue demands.

Chain Development

More chain retailers added to venue portfolio – media network reached 60,000 kiosks!

Going Digital @ Retail

Digital hand sanitizing wipe billboards launch to provide real-time targetability in select locations.

Continued Network Engagement

The Terraboost ad network reaches 70,000 locations – hand delivering 6 Billion Impressions per month! Major sales offices open in Las Vegas, NV and Indianapolis, IN as part of growth and commitment to customer service – bringing valued employee count to 150.

Omnichannel

Terraboost becomes omnichannel to improve customers experience with the wellness billboard medium and to further boost results for our valued customers on the local, regional, and enterprise level.
